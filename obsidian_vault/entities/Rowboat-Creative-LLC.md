# Rowboat Creative, LLC

50/50 member-managed Delaware LLC operating at 4200 W. Diversey Avenue, Chicago, IL. Screen printing, embroidery, and merchandising company. ASI: 83710. Co-founded by [[LG|Lucas Guariglia]] (CEO/50%) and [[JZ|Joseph Zangrilli]] (50%). JZ built the screen-printing operation from his basement; LG joined as sales partner with $10,000 loan to buy out JZ's prior partner.

Peak revenue of $2.3M (2019, 2021, 2022). Revenue collapsed to $46K in 2025 following the February 2023 partnership breakdown, February 2024 receivership, and May 2024 liquidation. Case No. 2024CH00720 in Cook County Chancery Division.
