# Spark Business Consulting

Accounting firm engaged by [[Rowboat-Creative-LLC]] during 2022-2023. Succeeded [[PKF Mueller / Cherry Bekaert]] (tax-only) and Rudder Services. Key personnel: Stephanie Morin and Sheri Highland.
