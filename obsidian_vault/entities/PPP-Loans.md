# PPP Loans (Paycheck Protection Program)

Rowboat Creative LLC received two PPP loans totaling $303,950.00 plus a $10,000 EIDL advance from the Small Business Administration (SBA), all processed through Fifth Third Bank. Both loans were certified as fully forgiven.

## Analysis

The 36-day gap between PPP1 deposit (4/20/2020) and the $70K diversion to LG's personal account (5/26/2020) is significant. The PPP funds were certified for payroll, rent, mortgage interest, and utilities — not for personal real estate purchases. The LG characterization of the $70K as "loan repayment" contradicts the absence of any documented personal loan to the company. The missing #4700 statements (source account for both the $70K transfer and $67,522 CASH DISB) represent a critical evidence gap.

Both loans being 100% forgiven raises questions about certification accuracy, particularly given the evidence of retroactive payroll date manipulation in the PPP USAGE spreadsheet and the documented large-scale diversion of PPP funds to personal use within five weeks of receipt.
