# Susan "Len" Mayersky

Fifth Third Bank representative who cultivated the banking relationship with [[LG]] exclusively, excluding 50% partner [[JZ]]. Referred to as "Len" in evidence. Visited [[Rowboat-Creative-LLC]] with "Steve" on May 17, 2019 to discuss Fifth Third banking solutions.
