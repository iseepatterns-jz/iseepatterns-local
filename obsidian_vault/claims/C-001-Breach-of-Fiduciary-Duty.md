# Claim C-001: Breach of Fiduciary Duty

## Summary
Lucas Guariglia, as 50% co-managing member of Rowboat Creative LLC, breached his fiduciary duties of loyalty and care by: excluding co-member Joseph Zangrilli from financial accounts, self-dealing through undisclosed side businesses, diverting corporate opportunities to his own entities, and making material misrepresentations to third parties about JZ.

## Legal Basis
- Delaware LLC Act § 18-1104 (fiduciary duties of members in member-managed LLCs)
- Duty of loyalty: refrain from self-dealing, usurpation of corporate opportunities
- Duty of care: manage with the care of an ordinarily prudent person

## Factual Allegations
1. LG demanded more than 50% ownership despite 50/50 operating agreement (Feb 2023, EXH-0006)
2. LG opened bank accounts excluding JZ, made himself sole signatory
3. LG operated undisclosed side businesses (China ~$30K, Layto, Goose Island $40K)
4. LG diverted Rowboat customer to AWM entity during active receivership (Apr 2024, EXH-0049)
5. LG took QuickBooks offline before receivership, obstructing financial transparency
6. LG excluded JZ from all financial decision-making (2022-2024)
