# Claim C-008: Tortious Interference with Business Relations

## Summary
LG tortiously interfered with Rowboat Creative's business relations by: (1) diverting Rowboat customers to his own entity AWM/All World Merch during active receivership (Apr 2024); (2) making false statements to contractor Abel Rodriguez that JZ "wants to shut down" and is "taking customer money" — damaging JZ's business relationships; (3) undermining JZ's standing with employees, vendors, and customers.

## Legal Basis
- Tortious interference with prospective economic advantage
- Tortious interference with contractual relations
- Prima facie elements: (1) valid business expectancy, (2) defendant's knowledge, (3) intentional interference, (4) proximate causation, (5) damages

## Factual Allegations
1. During receivership, Brand Addition asked to refund Rowboat order and re-place with AWM — at LG's direction (EXH-0049)
2. LG told Abel Rodriguez that JZ "wants to shut down," is "not right in his head," is "taking customer money" (EXH-0048)
3. LG diverted GoDaddy domain and payroll, disrupting customer communications
4. $150K and $500K customer jobs identified during receivership
