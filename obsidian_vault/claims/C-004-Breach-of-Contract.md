# Claim C-004: Breach of Contract / LLC Operating Agreement

## Summary
LG breached the Rowboat Creative LLC Operating Agreement — a 50/50 member-managed entity — by unilaterally seizing financial control, demanding majority ownership, excluding JZ from management decisions, opening bank accounts without JZ, and refusing to share financial information. The operating agreement (whether written or implied from course of dealing) required equal management rights.

## Legal Basis
- Delaware LLC Act: freedom of contract; operating agreement governs
- 6 Del. C. § 18-1101: Operating agreement enforcement
- Implied covenant of good faith and fair dealing

## Factual Allegations
1. LG demanded >50% ownership in violation of 50/50 structure (Feb 2023)
2. LG opened 8 new RBC accounts — JZ on none (EXH-0030)
3. LG took QuickBooks offline, denying JZ financial access
4. LG made unilateral decisions about payroll, OT policy, and client pricing
5. LG refused to engage in joint account search (Feb-Apr 2023)

## Damages
- Loss of management rights and equal control
- Financial harm from unilateral decisions
- Forced dissolution of the company
