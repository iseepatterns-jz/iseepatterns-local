# Claim C-011: Receivership Abuse / Post-Receivership Diversion

## Summary
During active court-ordered receivership (Feb 2024 – Mar 2025), LG: (1) diverted Rowboat customer Brand Addition to his own entity AWM/All World Merch (Apr 2024); (2) received $251K in distributions post-dispute while the estate was being liquidated; (3) took QuickBooks offline before receivership, obstructing the receiver's investigation; (4) now seeks voluntary dismissal without prejudice despite receiver finding $150K and $500K customer jobs during the receivership period.

## Legal Basis
- Contempt of court: violation of receivership orders
- Fraud on the court: concealing assets and business opportunities
- Breach of duty to cooperate with receiver
- Delaware LLC Act: prohibition on self-dealing during dissolution

## Factual Allegations
1. TRO hearing Feb 14, 2024 — receiver appointed (EXH-0043)
2. LG took QuickBooks offline before receiver could access (EXH-0045)
3. During receivership (Apr 25, 2024): Brand Addition asked to refund Rowboat order and re-place with AWM (EXH-0049)
4. Receiver Hayes confirmed $150K and $500K jobs with Rowboat customers (EXH-0058)
5. Estate "close to zeroed out" yet LG received $251K
6. LG seeks voluntary dismissal without prejudice (Mar 2025)
