# Claim C-006: PPP Loan Fraud

## Summary
Rowboat Creative received approximately $195,000 in PPP loans in 2020. Evidence suggests that $70,000 of PPP funds were diverted as a down payment on LG's personal residence (the "Dunkirk house") rather than being used for eligible payroll and business expenses as certified in the loan applications.

## Legal Basis
- False Claims Act: submission of false certifications to obtain federal funds
- 18 U.S.C. § 1343 (wire fraud): transmission of false PPP certifications via wire
- PPP loan program requirements: funds must be used for payroll and eligible business expenses

## Factual Allegations
1. Rowboat Creative received ~$195K in PPP loans (2020)
2. $70K used as down payment on LG's personal Dunkirk house
3. LG certified PPP funds would be used for eligible expenses
4. LG controlled all financial accounts at time of PPP applications
