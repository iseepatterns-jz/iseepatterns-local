# Claim C-005: Unjust Enrichment

## Summary
LG was unjustly enriched through: (1) operation of undisclosed side businesses using Rowboat resources, relationships, and goodwill; (2) receipt of $251K in post-dispute distributions while JZ received no salary; (3) diversion of Rowboat customers to his personal entity AWM/All World Merch during active receivership; and (4) price-fixing collusion with SG at Constellation Brands.

## Legal Basis
- Unjust enrichment: (1) enrichment of defendant, (2) at plaintiff's expense, (3) under circumstances making it unjust to retain
- Constructive trust over misappropriated assets
- Delaware restitution principles

## Factual Allegations
1. China side business operated using Rowboat resources and customer relationships
2. Layto and Goose Island $40K "parking" — company funds held in LG side entities
3. AWM/All World Merch received diverted Rowboat customer orders during receivership
4. $251K LG distributions post-dispute vs. JZ zero salary
5. Constellation price-fixing scheme enriched SG/Rowboat through improper means
