# Claim C-003: Fraud / Identity Theft (Bank of America Account)

## Summary
A fraudulent Bank of America account was opened in JZ's name on February 6, 2023 — an act of identity theft that subsequently triggered Fifth Third Bank to close all Rowboat Creative and JZ personal accounts (February 9, 2023). The timing and circumstances strongly suggest LG orchestrated or facilitated the identity theft to consolidate exclusive banking control. Later, payroll payments were blocked with an R29 fraud filter code.

## Legal Basis
- 18 U.S.C. § 1028: Fraud and related activity in connection with identification documents
- 18 U.S.C. § 1344: Bank fraud
- State identity theft and fraud statutes

## Factual Allegations
1. Bank of America account fraudulently opened in JZ's name (Feb 6, 2023)
2. Fifth Third closed all accounts 3 days later, citing the BoA identity theft as cause
3. Early Warning Services independently confirmed identity theft as likely cause (EXH-0007)
4. LG used account closures to justify sole-control Fifth Third setup
5. Payroll payment of $16,061.80 blocked with R29 fraud code (Jan 2024, EXH-0040)
